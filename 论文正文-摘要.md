# 摘要与关键词

## 中文摘要

高校党建工作长期面临党员发展流程不透明、组织管理分散、活动记录手工化、材料归档混乱、缺乏量化评估手段等实际痛点。党员发展从入党积极分子确定到正式党员转正需经历四个标准阶段，各环节的考察材料和时间节点分散在纸质档案和零散电子文档中，党组织难以实时掌握每位积极分子的培养进度。组织架构方面，党委、党总支、党支部三级之间的成员隶属关系缺乏统一的信息化平台维护，党员跨支部的组织关系转移依赖线下审批流转，审批周期长且过程状态不可追溯。党建活动开展过程中，会议签到、志愿活动参与等数据依靠人工事后统计汇总，不仅耗时耗力而且容易产生统计误差。入党申请书、思想汇报、政审函调材料、转正申请书等各类发展材料的归档工作量大且版本管理混乱，查找历史材料时效率低下。更为关键的是，现有管理模式无法对党员的活动参与度、学习活跃度和成长轨迹进行量化评估，党支部在确定重点培养对象和发展人选时缺乏客观数据支撑。

为系统性地解决上述问题，设计并实现了一套基于前后端分离B/S架构的智慧党建综合管理系统。后端采用Spring Boot 2.7.2框架作为核心服务引擎，集成MyBatis-Plus 3.5.2 ORM框架简化数据库访问层开发，通过Spring Data Redis实现分布式会话管理保障多实例部署时登录状态的一致性。前端选用Vue 3.2.13单页应用框架结合TypeScript 4.5.5类型约束构建交互界面，使用Ant Design Vue 4.2.6组件库提供表单、表格、树形选择器等UI元素，ECharts 6.0承担量化统计数据的图表渲染职责。MySQL 8.x数据库（数据库名party_building）按业务域划分存储16张核心数据表的全部结构化业务数据，Redis服务器（localhost:6379，database索引2）承载Session序列化数据，腾讯云COS对象存储服务接收并托管用户头像、材料文件和活动二维码等二进制资源。

系统功能划分为七大业务模块。用户管理模块实现基于MD5加密的身份认证和四种角色（超级管理员、组织管理员、党员、积极分子/发展对象）的RBAC注解式权限控制。党组织管理模块通过递归查询算法维护党委—党总支—党支部三级树形层级结构，提供组织负责人绑定和组织关系转移的三态审批流程（待审批/已通过/已拒绝）。发展阶段模块跟踪党员从入党积极分子到正式党员的全流程轨迹，以AOP切面机制为核心技术亮点自动捕获创建、更新、提交审核、审核和删除五类操作的变更前后数据快照并持久化至日志表。活动管理模块构建待发布、已发布、进行中、已结束四态生命周期模型，支持扫码签到和手动签到两种方式并能自动计算参与率和签到率统计指标。材料管理模块实现模板分类管理、六态提交流转（submitted→under_review→approved/rejected→final_approved→archived）和支部初审加超级管理员终审的两级审核机制，终审通过后触发自动归档操作。公告管理模块覆盖发布撤回、置顶排序、批量删除和基于中间表的已读追踪计数功能。量化统计模块从活动和材料两张上游表聚合计算参与率、签到率和材料完成率三个核心指标，通过ECharts柱状图、折线图和雷达图分别呈现管理员视角的组织维度报表和个人视角的多维评估结果。经黑盒功能测试验证，全部七大模块的核心业务逻辑运转正常，各模块间的数据流转和状态转换符合设计预期。

**关键词：**智慧党建；Spring Boot；Vue.js；AOP切面；党员发展全流程管理

## Abstract

Party building work in colleges and universities has long faced a series of practical challenges that hinder the efficiency and quality of daily operations. The party member development process, spanning four standard stages from activist determination to full member regularization, distributes assessment materials and timeline milestones across paper archives and scattered electronic documents, making it difficult for party organizations to track the training progress of each activist in real time. In terms of organizational architecture, the membership affiliation relationships among party committees, general party branches, and party branches lack a unified information platform for maintenance, and cross-branch organizational relationship transfers rely on offline approval workflows characterized by lengthy cycles and non-traceable process states. During party building activity operations, data points such as meeting attendance check-ins and volunteer activity participation are aggregated through manual post-hoc statistical compilation, which is not only time-consuming and labor-intensive but also prone to statistical discrepancies. The archiving workload for various development materials including party application letters, thought reports, political investigation documents, and regularization applications is substantial with chaotic version management, resulting in low efficiency when retrieving historical records. More critically, existing management paradigms cannot quantitatively evaluate indicators such as member activity participation rates, learning engagement levels, and growth trajectories, leaving party branches without objective data support when identifying key training targets and development candidates.

To systematically address the aforementioned challenges, a smart party building comprehensive management system based on a front-end and back-end separated B/S architecture was designed and implemented. The backend employs Spring Boot 2.7.2 as the core service engine, integrating MyBatis-Plus 3.5.2 as the ORM framework to simplify database access layer development, while Spring Data Redis handles distributed session management to ensure login state consistency across multi-instance deployment scenarios. The frontend utilizes Vue 3.2.13 single-page application framework combined with TypeScript 4.5.5 type constraints to build interactive interfaces, leveraging Ant Design Vue 4.2.6 component library for UI elements including forms, tables, and tree selectors, with ECharts 6.0 responsible for rendering quantitative statistical data visualizations. The MySQL 8.x database (named party_building) persistently stores structured business data across 16 core tables organized by business domain, while the Redis server (localhost:6379, database index 2) hosts serialized session data, and Tencent Cloud COS object storage service receives and hosts binary resources including user avatars, material files, and activity QR codes.

System functionality is organized into seven major business modules. The user management module implements MD5-encrypted identity authentication and annotation-based RBAC access control across four role types: super administrator, organization administrator, party member, and activist/development candidate. The organization management module maintains a three-level tree hierarchy structure (party committee, general party branch, party branch) through recursive query algorithms, providing organization leader binding and a three-state approval workflow (pending/approved/rejected) for organizational relationship transfers. The development stage module tracks the full lifecycle trajectory of party members from activists to formal members, featuring AOP aspect-oriented programming as its core technical innovation to automatically capture before-and-after data snapshots of five operation types—creation, update, submission for audit, audit execution, and deletion—and persist them to the log table. The activity management module constructs a four-state lifecycle model (draft, published, in-progress, concluded), supporting both QR code scanning and manual sign-in methods while automatically calculating participation rate and sign-in rate statistical metrics. The material management module implements template categorization management, a six-state submission workflow (submitted, under review, approved/rejected, final approved, archived), and a two-level audit mechanism combining branch-level initial review by organization administrators with final review by super administrators, triggering automatic archiving upon final approval success. The announcement management module covers publishing and withdrawal operations, pinning prioritization, batch deletion, and read-tracking count functionality based on an intermediate table. The quantitative statistics module aggregates three core metrics—participation rate, sign-in rate, and material completion rate—from upstream activity and material tables, presenting administrator-perspective organizational dimension reports and individual-perspective multi-dimensional assessment results through ECharts bar charts, line charts, and radar charts respectively. Black-box functional testing confirms that core business logic across all seven modules operates correctly, with inter-module data flows and state transitions aligning with design expectations.

**Keywords:** Smart Party Building; Spring Boot; Vue.js; AOP Aspect-Oriented Programming; Full-Lifecycle Party Member Development Management
